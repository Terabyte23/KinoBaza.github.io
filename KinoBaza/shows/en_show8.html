Silicon Valley

rating: 8.0 / 10 year: 2014 directors: Mike Judge, Alec Berg, Jamie Babbit, Eric Appel

Description of Silicon Valley

The main character of the series is Richard Hendricks, an extremely talented programmer working at one of the largest internet companies, who is developing his own music website. He manages to create a lossless music compression technology that has no competitors. Hendricks receives several enticing offers from millionaires in Silicon Valley: one offers to buy the rights to the product for 10 million dollars, while another offers to invest 200,000 in the site's development. Richard chooses the second offer, thus becoming the head of a promising startup that he will work on with a few other programmers. The only problem is that while they are good programmers, they know absolutely nothing about business.
