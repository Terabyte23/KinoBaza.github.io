Cloud Atlas

rating: 7.4 / 10 year: 2013 directors: Lana Wachowski, Tom Tykwer, Lilly Wachowski

Description of Cloud Atlas

The film describes six different stories that take place at different times but are closely interconnected. The past and the future intertwine; each choice and action leads to consequences that determine fate and the course of events in life. It all starts with an ordinary shepherd, then the action is constantly shifted from the 19th century to the future, which has undergone numerous disasters. Along with the main characters, who will continually transform into different identities, you will experience six life stories: the story of American lawyer Adam Ewing, who saves a black slave from death, who in turn will also save his life; the story of young composer Robert Frobisher, who, having run away from university, goes to work for the famous composer Vyvyan Ayrs; the story of a girl named Luisa, who works as a journalist, caught up in a dangerous investigation; the story of an elderly publisher being pursued by the criminal world; the story of a clone girl named Sonmi, who gets involved in a revolutionary movement; and the story of Zachry from the world of the future.
