The X-Files

rating: 9.3 / 10 year: 2008 director: Chris Carter

Description of The X-Files

After many years of collaboration, special FBI agents from the "X-Files" division, Dana Scully and Fox Mulder, left their positions for different reasons. The former became a staff physician at a Catholic hospital, while the latter remained obsessed with paranormal phenomena, collecting newspaper clippings and writing provocative articles. Both were given the opportunity to work together again when FBI agent Dakota Whitney sought their help. They needed to track down a serial killer of women, and the only person who could assist them in this case was a former priest, Father Joseph Crissman, who had previously served time for seducing minors and now claimed to have received the gift of clairvoyance.
